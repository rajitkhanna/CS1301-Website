Introduction:

J. Cole, born Jaermaine Lamarr Cole, is an American rapper. He hails from Fayetteville, North Carolina, a city he "put on the map." In 2011, his debut album went number one on the U.S. Billboard 200 and went platinum, a remarkable feat for freshman album. His next two albums Born Sinner in 2013 and Forest Hills Drive in 2014 also went platinum without any features. Forest Hills Drive earned a Grammy Award nomination for Best Rap Album. In 2016, Cole released 4 Your Eyez Only which debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and went platinum. His latest album KOD, an acronym for "Kids on Drugs" dropped in 2018 and was his fifth album to reach number one on the charts.
